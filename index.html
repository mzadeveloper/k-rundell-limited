Welcome To K Rundell Building Services

We will provide you with an impressive range of quality services, to help your project from idea to completion.

We redesign and refurbish shops, restaurants, houses, offices, industrial sheds and units. K Rundell will design your premises using the best standard of products and licensed tradespeople at an affordable price tailored to suit your budget.

We take the worry out of organising a project, from tradesmen to materials, we handle the lot. We will organise and employ all the trades, get the quotes and organise timeframes and you end up with the finished product, stress free!

From painters, plumbers, interior designers to tilers, air conditioning specialists and glazers… we have an extensive range of tradesmen to complete any sized project, on time, within budget to the best standards.
